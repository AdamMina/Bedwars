Bedwars

About Minecraft Bedwars

Minecraft Bedwars is a popular PvP (Player vs Player) mini-game where players or teams compete to destroy each other's beds while protecting their own. If your bed is destroyed, you can no longer respawn, making it essential to defend your bed while trying to eliminate your opponents.

How to Play

  • Gather resources from generators on your island.
  • Buy weapons, armor, and blocks from the shop.
  • Build bridges to other islands to attack and defend.
  • Destroy enemy beds to eliminate their respawn ability.
  • Last team standing wins!
